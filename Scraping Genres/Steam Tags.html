Steam tags are descriptive labels that help you discover games perfectly suited to your interests. Whether you're searching for indie platformers, open-world RPGs, or cozy simulation games, Steam's tagging system makes it easy to find exactly what you're looking for.

Every game on Steam can have up to 20 visible tags that describe its genre, visual style, themes, and gameplay features. These tags are applied by developers and the Steam community, creating a comprehensive system that accurately represents what each game offers.

From precision platformers to atmospheric horror games, tags help categorize the vast Steam library into meaningful, searchable segments that benefit both players looking for their next favorite game and developers wanting to reach their target audience.
